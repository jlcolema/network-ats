What is a Network Assessment?

A network assessment is a review of your organization’s existing IT infrastructure, management, security, processes, and performance. It can help provide actionable insights into underused/overused resources, bandwidth bottlenecks, security holes, and potential advantages and pitfalls in the rollout of new technologies.

A network assessment is an essential exercise for all networks, but there are three scenarios where it is critical:

  • Before a large roll-out that is expected to change your network structure.
  • If you have aging technology infrastructure that may be nearing its end-of-support or end-of-life.
  • Any time you suspect there are fundamental issues with your network or if you are unsure of how components of the network are actually functioning.

With a thorough assessment in-hand, you can quickly develop a plan for improving any-and-all aspects of your IT infrastructure.
